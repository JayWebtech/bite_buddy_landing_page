About BiteBuddy

BiteBuddy is a revolutionary NFT game that transforms your daily meals into an exciting adventure. By combining blockchain technology with AI-powered nutrition tracking, we're making healthy eating fun and rewarding.

Your digital pet grows and evolves based on your real-world eating habits. Each meal you scan becomes a unique NFT, contributing to your pet's strength and abilities. Battle other players, collect rare NFTs, and join a community of health-conscious gamers.
